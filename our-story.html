Hello,

  • Home Page

    Initially, our team grappled with conflicting ideas for the homepage design. One proposed a complex layout, challenging to implement across subtopic pages, while another suggested a simpler, modern design. Resolving this, we prioritized functionality and consistency. We chose the simpler design, aligning with contemporary standards and ensuring seamless integration across pages. This decision emphasized the importance of cohesive design and taught us to prioritize practicality without compromising aesthetic appeal.

    Scripting and Registration errors

    Upon publishing three subtopic pages for student continuation, an obstacle emerged during the integration of registered usernames/first names into the navigation bar. Initially, we attempted to incorporate the function within a script on each HTML page. However, a significant issue surfaced: the called-out name didn't align with the navigation list in the CSS file. Consequently, the displayed names appeared as unstyled text, devoid of any design. This persistent challenge prompted a critical reevaluation of our strategy. We aimed to find a solution that would seamlessly integrate and stylize the usernames/first names within the navigation bar. Our focus shifted toward exploring alternative methods to ensure a cohesive design across all pages. This hurdle underscored the importance of meticulous coding and design alignment, emphasizing the need for a robust integration strategy to maintain consistency and aesthetic appeal throughout the website.

  • Group meetings

    In dividing our work, we allocated each subtopic to a different student. Throughout the webpage creation, we orchestrated regular group meetings to review and discuss individual progress, ensuring all tasks were completed. These meetings convened twice a week on campus, dedicating each week to a specific page. Once a page was finished, attention shifted to another student's page, fostering a collaborative work dynamic. Additionally, beyond these scheduled meetings, we established an open communication channel. Whenever a student encountered a problem with their work, an immediate online call was initiated, gathering the team to troubleshoot and assist in rectifying script or page issues. This proactive approach ensured swift resolution of individual challenges, maintaining the project's momentum and preventing roadblocks from impeding progress. The combination of structured group meetings for collective progress assessment and impromptu online calls for immediate issue resolution fostered a supportive and efficient work environment, promoting both individual responsibility and team collaboration in successfully navigating the intricacies of webpage creation.

    Solving problems

    When a student faced coding issues without real-time help, our team employed a collaborative solution. The troubled student shared their HTML/CSS/JavaScript file in our group chat. This allowed the next team member to review the code at their convenience, identifying errors and suggesting improvements. This practice fostered ongoing learning, encouraging knowledge sharing and skill enhancement. Each reviewer not only fixed immediate issues but also provided suggestions for better coding practices. This collective effort enriched our understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, fostering a supportive environment for skill development and knowledge exchange.

  • Functionality

    Through our group collaboration in this project, we gained valuable insights into proficient web page design. We learned to adeptly link scripts within pages, enhancing functionality for both scriptwriters and page browsers. This experience honed our skills in crafting pages that seamlessly integrate scripts, optimizing functionality and user experience. It provided us with multifaceted approaches to elevate scripting techniques, ultimately enhancing the browsing experience while reinforcing our understanding of effective webpage design.

    Excecises

    In our learning process, we've discovered the art of constructing functional websites sans scripts in certain scenarios. Simultaneously, we've delved into data preservation techniques, establishing pathways to interlink this data, employing tools like "SessionStorage." Additionally, we've grasped the significance of loading functions, strategically implementing them during website launches. This experience showcased the versatility of building websites, from script-free functionalities to managing and correlating data through storage methods like "SessionStorage." Moreover, integrating loading functions at the website's initiation emerged as a pivotal strategy, underscoring our comprehension of optimizing user experiences through efficient loading processes.
